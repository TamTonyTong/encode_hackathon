# Free APIs for Future Development

Here is a curated list of free APIs that can be integrated into the Kitchen Assistant application for enhanced functionality.

## 1. Food & Recipes

### TheMealDB (Already Implemented)
- **URL**: [https://www.themealdb.com/api.php](https://www.themealdb.com/api.php)
- **Features**: Recipe search, ingredients, random meal, categories.
- **Cost**: Free (v1), Premium available.

### OpenFoodFacts
- **URL**: [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data)
- **Features**: Huge database of food products, includes barcodes, nutriscore, ingredients, allergens.
- **Use Case**: Barcode scanning feature for pantry management.
- **Cost**: Free & Open Source.

### TheCocktailDB
- **URL**: [https://www.thecocktaildb.com/api.php](https://www.thecocktaildb.com/api.php)
- **Features**: Drink recipes, ingredients, glass types.
- **Use Case**: Suggesting drink pairings for meals.
- **Cost**: Free (v1).

### Fryityvice
- **URL**: [https://www.fruityvice.com/](https://www.fruityvice.com/)
- **Features**: Fruit nutrition data.
- **Cost**: Free.

## 2. Nutrition & Health

### USDA FoodData Central
- **URL**: [https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/api-guide.html](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/api-guide.html)
- **Features**: Comprehensive nutritional data from the US Department of Agriculture.
- **Use Case**: Accurate calorie and macro calculation.
- **Cost**: Free (Key required).

### Edamam Nutrition Analysis
- **URL**: [https://developer.edamam.com/edamam-nutrition-api](https://developer.edamam.com/edamam-nutrition-api)
- **Features**: Text analysis for nutrition (e.g., "1 cup of rice" -> nutrition).
- **Cost**: Freemium (generous free tier).

## 3. Product & Shopping

### Fake Store API
- **URL**: [https://fakestoreapi.com/](https://fakestoreapi.com/)
- **Features**: Mock e-commerce data (products, carts, users).
- **Use Case**: Testing the grocery store UI/UX without real inventory.
- **Cost**: Free.

### UPCitemdb
- **URL**: [https://www.upcitemdb.com/api/explorer](https://www.upcitemdb.com/api/explorer)
- **Features**: Lookup product info by UPC code.
- **Cost**: Freemium.

## 4. AI & Tools

### Hugging Face Inference API
- **URL**: [https://huggingface.co/docs/api-inference/index](https://huggingface.co/docs/api-inference/index)
- **Features**: Access to thousands of open-source AI models (Vision, Text, Classification).
- **Cost**: Free tier available.

### Pexels / Unsplash / Pixabay
- **URL**: [https://www.pexels.com/api/](https://www.pexels.com/api/)
- **Features**: Free stock photos.
- **Use Case**: Placeholder images for categories or generic food items.
- **Cost**: Free.
